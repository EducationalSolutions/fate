The three dimensional nature of our atmosphere often makes tracking airborne pollutants very difficult. This section of Fate allows you to estimate the concentration of a pollutant at a specific distance downwind from an instantaneous release of pollutant. An example of a source that might release this type of pollutant puff is the explosion of an ammonia tank.
